First Professional Experience at Safran Cabine: Stepping into Leadership

Just one month into my role at Safran Cabine, I was given the incredible opportunity to join a Black Belt project—the very first of its kind at Safran Tunisia. This experience allowed me to learn from seasoned professionals, and when the project succeeded, it significantly boosted my managers' confidence in my abilities. From that point, I began leading projects—starting with smaller ones and progressing to more complex challenges.

Alongside this, I was also a member of another successful Green Belt Lean Six Sigma project. Safran’s DNA is deeply rooted in innovation, and being passionate about it, I collaborated with teams from Safran Cabin Czech to replicate projects, benchmark initiatives, and generate fresh ideas together.

Becoming a PRAP Referent: A New Responsibility

In 2019, I earned my certification as a "Prevention of Risks Related to Physical Activity" (PRAP) practitioner, accredited by INRS France. The certification process, guided by a group referent, was truly life-changing. I became the first and only PRAP referent at Safran Cabine Tunisia, which brought the responsibility of working on all the ergonomics-related projects. This new role pushed me to develop further in the field of safety and ergonomics.

Leading the Dynamic Line Project: A Turning Point

One key project I was involved in was the "Dynamic Line" for galleys assembly. The project aimed to enhance quality, reduce lead times, and, most importantly, improve ergonomics and security for the team. The project was a finalist for the Safran Innovation Awards—a testament to the hard work and dedication of the team. When the original project manager was promoted to another site in France, I stepped in as his backup.

This was a pivotal moment in my career. I took on full responsibility for project management, from planning to execution, and despite the challenges, I never felt exhausted. I was passionate about what I was doing, and this experience solidified my love for project management.
